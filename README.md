# Design System: Noor Serenity (Muslim Association of Woodstock)

## 1. Creative North Star: "The Sacred Sanctuary"
The visual identity of the Muslim Association of Woodstock is built on the concept of **The Sacred Sanctuary**. It aims to provide a digital experience that feels both rooted in timeless Islamic tradition and forward-looking in its modern execution. The design is intentional, spacious, and calming.

## 2. Brand Identity
- **Full Name:** Muslim Association of Woodstock
- **Voice & Tone:** Welcoming, professional, spiritual, and community-focused.
- **Key Message:** "Nurturing Faith, Building Community."

## 3. Visual Language

### Typography
Consistent typography is the cornerstone of our professional look.
- **Primary Serif (Headlines):** `Noto Serif`. This font provides a sense of history, authority, and elegance. Used for all major page headers and section titles.
- **Secondary Sans-Serif (Body):** `Manrope`. A modern, highly readable geometric sans-serif used for body copy, navigation, and UI elements to ensure accessibility and a clean feel.

### Color Palette
- **Deep Emerald (#064E3B):** Our primary brand color. It represents growth, peace, and the traditional color associated with Islam. Used for headers, primary buttons, and key UI accents.
- **Golden Accent (#735c00 / #fed65b):** Used sparingly to highlight important information, such as the "Active" prayer time or primary call-to-action buttons like "Donate".
- **Light Surface (#fcf9f1):** A warm, off-white background that prevents the harshness of pure white, contributing to the "Sanctuary" feel.
- **Tonal Neutrals (#1c1c17):** Used for primary text to ensure high contrast and readability.

### Layout & Spacing
- **Spaciousness:** Large margins and generous white space (negative space) are used to prevent information overload and create a sense of calm.
- **Grid System:** A structured grid ensures that content like "Daily Prayer Times" and "Programs" are organized and easy to scan.
- **Soft Roundness:** We use a subtle corner radius (approx. 8px-12px) on cards and buttons to make the interface feel approachable and modern rather than sharp or clinical.

## 4. Key UI Components
- **Top Navigation Bar:** Features the official logo, clear navigation links, and a high-visibility "Prayer Times" and "Donate" button set.
- **Hero Sections:** High-quality photography of the Woodstock mosque integrated with bold, Noto Serif typography.
- **Prayer Time Cards:** A clean, grid-based layout that highlights the current active prayer.
- **Footer:** A grounded, tonal-blocking section containing essential links, social icons, and the full association address.

## 5. Usage Guidelines
- Always use the **Full Name** "Muslim Association of Woodstock" in formal headers.
- Maintain the **Noto Serif** for headers and **Manrope** for body text to keep the "consistent" look requested.
- Ensure the **Primary Logo** is always placed on a clear background with sufficient padding.
