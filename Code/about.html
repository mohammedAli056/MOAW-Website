Our Journey

Nurturing Faith, Building Community.

The Muslim Association of Woodstock is a spiritual home dedicated to fostering a vibrant Islamic community through education, service, and devotion.

Who We Are

We are a diverse congregation of Muslims in Woodstock and the surrounding Oxford County area. Our association serves as a focal point for religious, educational, and social activities, welcoming people from all walks of life to experience the beauty of Islamic tradition.

Our Mission

"To establish a sacred space where the remembrance of God is central, where knowledge is shared freely, and where the prophetic example of compassion and service is manifested in our daily lives for the benefit of the greater Woodstock community."

History of Islamic Faith

Rooted in the timeless message revealed to the Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him), the Islamic faith has been a beacon of knowledge and justice for over 1,400 years.

Foundation

The Prophet's Era

The emergence of Islam in 7th-century Arabia, establishing a framework of monotheism, social justice, and communal responsibility.

Growth

The Golden Age

Centuries of unprecedented advancements in science, philosophy, and art, driven by the Quranic mandate to seek knowledge.

Islam in Woodstock

Building a bridge between ancient tradition and modern Canadian life, establishing our community as an integral part of Oxford County.
